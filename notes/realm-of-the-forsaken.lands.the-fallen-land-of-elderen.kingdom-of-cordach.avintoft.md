# Avintoft: The Crown Jewel of Cordach

Avintoft, the majestic capital of the Kingdom of Cordach, stands as a testament to the realm's prosperity and power. Nestled at the heart of the kingdom, this bustling metropolis is a hub of commerce, culture, and royal grandeur.

## Notable Locations

### The Hall of Cordach

The imposing The Hall of Cordach dominates the city skyline. This ancient fortress serves as both the residence of the royal family and the seat of government. Its towering spires and formidable walls are a constant reminder of the kingdom's strength and longevity.

### The Gilded Square

At the city's core lies the sprawling Gilded Square, a labyrinth of colorful stalls and shops. Here, merchants from across the realm and beyond peddle their wares, creating a vibrant tapestry of sights, sounds, and smells.

### North Ridge Trading Company Office

The North Ridge Trading Company maintains a prominent office in Avintoft, serving as a hub for their operations in the capital. While not as grand as a headquarters, this office is still a bustling center of activity, where traders and merchants conduct business crucial to the kingdom's economy.

### The Crimson Crow Tavern

Among the city's many taverns, the Crimson Crow stands out as a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Known for its excellent ale and mysterious atmosphere, it's said that the tavern's ancient crow statue can grant wishes to those who know how to listen.

### The Celestial Spire

This tall, slender tower houses the royal astronomers and serves as an observatory. Its unique design allows for unparalleled views of the night sky, making it a center for both scientific study and mystical divination.

## Quirks and Traditions

- The Cordachian Chime: Every hour, the great bell in the Celestial Spire rings out a unique melody. Citizens pause their activities to listen, believing that the tune foretells the city's fortune for the coming hour.
- The Whispering Trees: Avintoft is known for its ancient whispering willow trees. Locals tie ribbons with written wishes to the branches, believing the rustling leaves carry their desires to the gods.
- The Royal Reflection: Once a year, the monarch stands before a giant mirror in the town square. It's said that the reflection reveals the true nature of their rule, visible to all citizens.
- The Merchant's Thread: All shopkeepers in Avintoft wear a distinctive red thread around their wrist, symbolizing their oath to fair trade. Breaking this oath is said to cause the thread to unravel, bringing misfortune.
- The Starfall Feast: During the annual meteor shower, Avintoft hosts a citywide feast where tables are set out in the streets. It's believed that wishes made while sharing food under the falling stars are destined to come true.

With its rich history, vibrant culture, and strong ties to both royalty and commerce, Avintoft truly embodies the spirit of Cordach. The city's unique blend of tradition and innovation continues to attract visitors and new residents, ensuring its position as the beating heart of the kingdom.
