- Food and drink items seem to range from a few copper pieces (cp) to a few silver pieces (sp) for common items. For instance, a loaf of bread costs 2 cp, while a chunk of meat costs 3 sp.
- Lodging prices vary based on quality, ranging from 7 cp per day for squalid accommodations to 4 gp per day for aristocratic lodging.
- Transportation costs can be estimated. For example, a riding horse costs 75 gp, while a carriage costs 100 gp.
- Services have a wide range of prices. Skilled hirelings charge 2 gp per day, while untrained ones charge 2 sp per day.
- Magic items have a significant price range based on rarity, from 50-100 gp for common items to 50,000+ gp for legendary items.
