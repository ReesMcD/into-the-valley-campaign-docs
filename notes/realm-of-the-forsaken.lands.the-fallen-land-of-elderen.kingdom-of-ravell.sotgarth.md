- Key Information

Sotgarth is a small town nestled in the Kingdom of Ravell, known for its unique charm and local industry. Here are some key details about the town:

### Notable Locations

- **The Wyrm's Whisper Inn:** A cozy tavern named after the nearby Wyrm's Spine mountains. It's famous for its "Dragon's Breath Ale," a spicy brew that locals swear has healing properties.
- **Glimmer's Goods:** The town's general store, run by the eccentric halfling Fizzle Glimmergear. It's known for its eclectic mix of everyday items and peculiar trinkets from across Ravell.

### Local Trade

Sotgarth is renowned for its unique wool industry. The town raises a special breed of sheep called "Sotgarth Silkwools," whose fleece is prized for its incredibly soft texture and slight iridescent sheen.

### Quirks and Features

- The town has a tradition of painting their roofs in vibrant colors, said to ward off evil spirits and bring good luck.
- Every full moon, the townspeople gather in the central square for the "Moonlight Market," where they trade goods and share local gossip.
- Sotgarth is home to a small colony of friendly kobolds who have integrated into the town, often helping with mining in the nearby hills.
- The town boasts a natural hot spring on its outskirts, believed to have minor healing properties. It's a popular spot for both locals and travelers.

Despite its small size, Sotgarth plays a crucial role in Ravell's economy due to its unique wool trade and strategic location near the Wyrm's Spine mountains. The town's quirky traditions and diverse population make it a fascinating stop for those traveling through the kingdom.
