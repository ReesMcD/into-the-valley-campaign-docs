# Description

Kalnaer, a humble milling and lumber town that was once known for not much more than an inn, tavern, and general store, is now bustling with life as it prepares for the upcoming Festival of Light. The festival is a celebration of light, life, and peace and is held once every 10 years. It is one of the most sacred and historic traditions in Kalnaer, drawing visitors from far and wide to witness the festivities.

# History

Pinebach was founded due to its access to plentiful lumber and a kingdom of largely grasslands. This made it an ideal location for settlers to establish a thriving community.

Furthermore, Pinebach became home to the Festival of Light, an annual celebration that showcases the city's deep connection to religion and spirituality. It is particularly significant as it is home to the first Church of Pelor in the kingdom, attracting pilgrims and worshippers from far and wide.

However, not all was peaceful in Pinebach. The Obyrith Order, a notorious dark cult, launched a devastating attack on the Festival of Light in the year 353 4E. In a shocking turn of events, they managed to abduct the Chosen Child of Kalnaer, a young individual destined for greatness.

# Locations in Pinebach:

## The Bronze Boar

The Bronze Boar is a small and humble tavern, owned and operated by the renowned [[realm-of-the-forsaken.npcs.irrelevent-npcs.mudmear-shadowbrow|Mudmear Shadowbrow]]. This charming establishment is known for its warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for weary travelers and locals alike.

As you approach the tavern, your eyes are immediately drawn to the large boar head hanging proudly in front of the door. This magnificent display symbolizes the tavern's name and adds a touch of rustic charm to the exterior.

Step inside The Bronze Boar and you will be greeted with the delightful aroma of hearty meals being prepared in the kitchen. The cozy interior is adorned with wooden furnishings, giving it a rustic and nostalgic feel. The walls are adorned with interesting artifacts and old paintings, each with its own story to tell.

Mudmear Shadowbrow, the owner and proprietor of The Bronze Boar, is known for his warm hospitality and friendly demeanor. He takes great pride in ensuring every guest feels welcome and comfortable during their visit. Whether you're looking for a relaxing evening with friends or a delicious meal to satisfy your appetite, Mudmear and his staff are always ready to provide a memorable experience.

## The Gluttonous Owlbear

The Gluttonous Owlbear is the one and only general store in Pinebach, a small town known for its charm and natural beauty.

One of the notable features of the store is a magnificent taxidermied Owlbear proudly displayed inside the shop. The Owlbear, with its striking appearance and intricate details, never fails to captivate the attention of visitors.

The friendly and knowledgeable owner of The Gluttonous Owlbear is always ready to assist customers with their inquiries and provide recommendations. Whether finding the perfect gift or suggesting the best local hiking trails, they go above and beyond to ensure a memorable shopping experience.

## Lumber Mill

The local lumber mill in Pinebach is a key player in the kingdom's lumber industry. Despite its small size, it is highly utilized and plays a crucial role in meeting the region's high demand for lumber. With its strategic location and efficient operations, the mill serves as a hub for processing and supplying lumber to various industries and construction projects within the kingdom. It creates jobs, supports the community, and has a reputation for producing top-notch lumber products. Overall, the mill is integral to the thriving lumber industry in the kingdom, meeting the ever-growing demand for this essential resource.

## Church of Pelor

The Church of Pelor, the oldest church in the Kingdom of Kalnaer, holds a significant role in the lives of its citizens. Founded centuries ago, it spread love, peace, and enlightenment. The church's notable contribution is the Festival of Light, a joyous occasion celebrating Pelor's blessings. Unfortunately, the recent attack on the festival left the church unoccupied, standing as a somber reminder of the need for rebuilding and restoration.

## Goblin Hideout

The Goblin Hideout is a small cave in Pinebach and serves as the home for a small tribe of goblins. These goblins are aligned with the ongoing goblin uprising in the kingdom, seeking to challenge the existing power structures and assert their own dominance.

Recently, there was an incident where the goblins briefly abducted Anders Shortwit, a professor researching the goblins. However, Anders managed to escape from his captors, showcasing his resourcefulness and bravery in the face of danger.
