- **Key Plot Lines**
    - The Circle of Spores is unleashing a plague on Egoleren in an effort to “restore” the natural order.
    - The cult of Kylor is taking over the throne as the king has fallen ill; they believe the kingdom's lack of faith is the reason they have fallen. Using their ability to cure the plague.

# Map

# Description

War torn country that has been the battle field of many wars. Most of its inhabitable land has been rendered useless from generations was war. It is home to the Great Egoleren Bogs which are considered a dangerous and unforgiving place. 

# History

- Run by the Church of Kylor, formerly House Blackburn
- Was the grounds for much of the War of Forn Valley
- Fraz-Urb’luu, **Fedele** in Common, a Demon lord from the Abyss, has taken loose control of Egoleren. He has deceived and charmed both the Circle of Spores and the Church of Kylor.
- Bullywugs live deep in the bogs of this land
