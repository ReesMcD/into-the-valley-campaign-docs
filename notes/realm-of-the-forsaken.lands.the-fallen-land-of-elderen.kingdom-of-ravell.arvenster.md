# Arvenster

Arvenster is a small, picturesque town nestled in the rolling hills of the Kingdom of Ravell. Known for its charming atmosphere and friendly inhabitants, Arvenster serves as a welcome respite for travelers and a tight-knit community for its residents.

## Key Locations

- **The Wyvern's Rest Inn:** A cozy establishment run by the jovial Bartleby Hornblower, famous for its spiced mead and hearty stews.
- **Thistledown General Store:** Owned by the eccentric twins, Fern and Flora Thistledown, this shop stocks an eclectic mix of everyday items and curious trinkets.
- **Arvenster Glassworks:** The town's primary trade, known throughout Ravell for its delicate, colorful glassware and intricate window panes.

## Notable Quirks

- **Annual Firefly Festival:** Every midsummer, the town releases thousands of fireflies, creating a magical light show.
- **The Whispering Well:** A mysterious well in the town square that's said to echo back secrets if you whisper into it at midnight.
- **Colorful Houses:** Residents paint their homes in vibrant hues to match the colors of their glassware, creating a rainbow-like townscape.

Arvenster's charm lies in its blend of quaint traditions and the creative spirit of its glassblowing artisans, making it a unique gem within the Kingdom of Ravell.
