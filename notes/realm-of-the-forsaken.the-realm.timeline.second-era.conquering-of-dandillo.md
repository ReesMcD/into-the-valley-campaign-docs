- After being pushed from Elderen, the Elves fled to Dandillo, a harsh and wild land where magical creatures were roaming free for ages.
- The last of the dragon riders escaped to Dandillo inhabiting the land of Obolo.
- It is not known how the Elves arrived to Dandillo as it was a land never settled or explored.
