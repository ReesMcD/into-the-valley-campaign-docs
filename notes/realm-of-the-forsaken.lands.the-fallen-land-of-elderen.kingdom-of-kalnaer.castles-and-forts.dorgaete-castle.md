# Description

- Castle built on  an elven tomb
- Many annexes that have been added to the original structure

# History

- Originally owned by the lord of Stonepost, he mysteriously disappeared.
- The Obyrith order raided the castle looking for the Eye of Orcus, which they found
- The Firenewts eventually took control after the Obyrith Orders wake, but they were defeated by the Nights of Kale
