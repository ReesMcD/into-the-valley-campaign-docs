# 1. Boots of Dramatic Entrance

These boots create a gust of wind and a flurry of leaves whenever you enter a room, regardless of the season or indoor location.

# 2. Endless Handkerchief

Pull this handkerchief from your pocket, and it keeps coming. Useful for cleaning up messes, makeshift rope, or impromptu magic shows.

# 3. Spoon of Temperature Testing

This spoon changes color based on the temperature of the food or liquid it touches. Never burn your tongue again!

# 6. Wand of Smiles

Point this wand at someone, and they involuntarily smile for a few seconds. Perfect for lightening the mood or really awkward diplomatic situations.

# 7. Boots of False Tracks

These boots leave animal tracks instead of footprints. Choose the animal when you attune to the boots.

# 8. Pitcher of Endless Water

This pitcher never runs out of fresh water, but it only pours when tilted exactly 45 degrees.

# 9. Compass of Indecision

This compass points to whatever the holder is thinking about most at that moment. Not always reliable, but occasionally insightful.

# 10. Mood Ring of Truth

This ring changes color based on the wearer's true emotions, regardless of what they're trying to project.

# 12. Plate of Reheating

Place any food on this plate, and it instantly warms to the perfect serving temperature.

# 13. Quill of Dictation

This quill writes down everything said within 30 feet of it, but with random words replaced by their sillier synonyms.

# 14. Bracelet of Mosquito Warding

Keeps mosquitoes away from the wearer in a 5-foot radius. Side effect: you smell faintly of citronella.

# 15. Goggles of Night Blindness

These goggles grant darkvision, but make you completely blind in daylight.

# 16. Coin of Probability

This coin always lands on its edge when flipped. Great for avoiding decisions or confusing statisticians.

# 17. Bedroll of Comfort

This bedroll always feels like the most comfortable bed you've ever slept in, regardless of the terrain.

# 20. Helmet of Hairdo Preservation

Your hairstyle remains perfect no matter what, even after removing the helmet.

# 21. Gloves of Fingerprinting

These gloves leave someone else's fingerprints on everything you touch. The fingerprints change daily.

# 22. Mirror of Confidence

Your reflection in this mirror always winks and gives you a thumbs up, boosting your confidence.

# 24. Tablecloth of Cleanliness

Food and drink spills instantly disappear from this tablecloth. However, it also occasionally eats utensils.
