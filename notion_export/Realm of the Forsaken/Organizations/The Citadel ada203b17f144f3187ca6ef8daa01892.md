# The Citadel University of Illara

*“Knowledge Through Wonder, Wisdom Through Discovery”*

## Leadership

**Arch-Dean Celestine Starweaver** *(High Elf)*

- Supreme head of all colleges and departments
- 847 years old, specializes in Temporal Magic Theory
- Known for her floating office that moves throughout the campus
- Famous for her crystalline spectacles that can see magical auras

## Notable Professors

### Existing Faculty

- **Anders Shortwit** - Monster Research and Relations *(Young Human)*
- **Gladis Saword** - Elven History and Studies *(Half Elf)*
- **Bravis Nimbleweed** - Cult Research and Studies *(Halfling)*
- **Nigel Pennyworth** - Archeology *(Human)*
- **Elandor Brightspear** - Planar Travel and Teleportation *(Half Elf)*

### Additional Notable Faculty

**Professor Thaddeus Boomwright** *(Gnome)* - Advanced Alchemical Sciences

- Missing three fingers from various “successful” experiments
- His laboratory produces colorful smoke that changes with his mood

**Dr. Seraphina Moonwhisper** *(Tiefling)* - Healing Arts and Magical Medicine

- Dean of the Medical College
- Can diagnose illnesses by tasting a patient’s aura
- Her office garden grows rare medicinal herbs that sing when ready for harvest

**Professor Grimjaw Ironquill** *(Dwarf)* - Practical Enchantment and Artificery

- Renowned for creating the university’s self-writing quills
- His beard contains tiny tools and often sparks during lectures
- Students swear his hammer can fix both broken items and broken hearts

**Madame Lyralei Stardustbottom** *(Halfling)* - Bardic Performance and Sonic Magic

- Dean of the Bard College
- Her voice can literally move mountains (she’s banned from singing above a whisper in the library)
- Travels to class in a musical chair that plays her entrance theme

**Professor Zephyr Cloudstrider** *(Air Genasi)* - Weather Magic and Atmospheric Studies

- Classes are held outdoors, weather permitting (which is always, since he controls it)
- Constantly hovers 3 feet off the ground
    - Head of the Department of Experimental Alchemy
- His office is a literal cloud anchored to the astronomy tower

## Colleges and Departments

### College of Arcane Studies

**Dean:** Professor Eldara Voidtouch *(Drow)*

- **Departments:** Evocation, Transmutation, Divination, Enchantment, Abjuration, Necromancy, Conjuration, Illusion
- **Notable Course:** “Fireball Trajectory and Collateral Damage Assessment”

### College of Natural Philosophy

**Dean:** Dr. Oakenheart Willowbend *(Firbolg)*

- **Departments:** Basic Alchemy, Herbology, Astronomy, Mathematical Theory
- **Notable Course:** “Applied Geometry in Dungeon Architecture”

### College of Historical and Cultural Studies

**Dean:** Professor Gladis Saword *(Half Elf)*

- **Departments:** Ancient Civilizations, Comparative Religion, Linguistic Studies, Anthropology
- **Notable Course:** “Deciphering Dead Languages Without Dying”

### College of Healing Arts

**Dean:** Dr. Seraphina Moonwhisper *(Tiefling)*

- **Departments:** Magical Medicine, Medicinal Potions Making, Anatomy, Psychology
- **Notable Course:** “Resurrection Ethics and Liability”

### Bard College of Melodic Arts

**Dean:** Madame Lyralei Stardustbottom *(Halfling)*

- **Departments:** Performance, Composition, Sound Magic, Cultural Arts
- **Notable Course:** “Weaponizing Show Tunes”

## Notable Locations

### Academic Buildings

**The Transmutation Tower**

- Changes architectural style every hour
- Houses advanced alchemy labs where catalysts are tested
- The basement occasionally becomes the top floor

**The Whispering Library**

- Books literally whisper their contents to help students study
- The Restricted Section requires a blood oath and three references
- Staffed by a phantom librarian who shushes people from beyond the grave

**The Observatory of Infinite Skies**

- Dome rotates to show any celestial body in any plane of existence
- Professor Cloudstrider’s office-cloud is anchored here
- Students often get vertigo from the planar viewing sessions

**Boomwright’s Experimental Laboratory**

- Reinforced with adamantine after the “Great Explosion of 1847”
- Alchemist supplies of legendary tier available for advanced students
- Has its own cleanup crew of summoned elementals

### Unique Facilities

**The Manifestation Chambers**

- Where theoretical magic becomes practical application
- Each room adapts to the type of magic being practiced
- Room 13 is permanently stuck as a tropical beach (no one complains)

**The Memory Vault**

- Stores important historical events as tangible memories
- Guarded by a sphinx who asks riddles about filing systems
- Students can literally experience historical events for their dissertations

**The Resonance Hall**

- Bard college’s main performance space
- Acoustics enhanced by sonic magic to carry music across dimensions
- The ghost of a famous composer occasionally hijacks performances

**The Greenhouse of Impossible Things**

- Grows plants from every known plane of existence
- Legendary flowers and rare mushrooms cultivated for alchemical studies
- Students need special permission and protective gear to enter certain sections

**The Dueling Grounds**

- Magically regulated combat training area
- Surrounded by healing stations staffed by medical students
- Damage to pride heals slower than physical wounds

### Student Life Areas

**The Common Crucible** (Student Union)

- Serves food that changes flavor based on your mood
- Chess sets play themselves while waiting for opponents
- Notice board posts itself and organizes messages by urgency

**Dormitories of the Four Elements**

- Fire Wing: Always perfectly warm, fireplaces in every room
- Water Wing: Soothing fountain sounds, occasional indoor rain
- Earth Wing: Rooms carved from living stone, very stable
- Air Wing: Rooms hover slightly off the ground, breezy

**The Midnight Market**

- Underground bazaar run by students selling ingredients and services
- Only open from 11 PM to 3 AM
- Currency includes favors, gossip, and rare spell components

## Quirky Traditions

**The Familiar Parade**

- Annual event where students show off their magical companions
- Professor Shortwit judges the “Most Unusual” category
- Last year’s winner was a student whose familiar was a tiny dragon that hiccupped portals

**Thesis Defense Duels**

- Graduate students must defend their thesis both intellectually and magically
- Healing stations are mandatory
- Extra credit for creative spell usage

**The Great Experiment**

- Once per semester, the entire university collaborates on one massive magical experiment
- Previous successes include creating a rainbow that rained different flavored waters
- Previous failures are classified but involve three missing courtyards

**Midnight Alchemy**

- Secret student society that brews potions after hours
- Their addiction to academic achievement is legendary
- Meet in the abandoned tower that may or may not exist on Tuesdays
