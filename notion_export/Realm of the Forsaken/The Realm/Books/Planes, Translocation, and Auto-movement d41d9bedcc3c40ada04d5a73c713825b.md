# Planes, Translocation, and Auto-movement

Tags: Book

By Elandor Brightspear

Greetings, esteemed readers. I am Elandor Brightspear, a professor at the Citadel and foremost researcher on planar travel. In this guide, we will explore the intricacies of planar travel and teleportation, delving into the various planes of existence and the ancient elven mastery of these arts.

The Material Plane:

Our journey begins with the Material Plane, the primary realm of mortal existence. This plane is the nexus of reality, where most adventures unfold and civilizations thrive. It is from here that we reach out to other planes of existence.

The Shadowfell and Feywild:

Two planes closely linked to the Material Plane are the Shadowfell and the Feywild. The Shadowfell is a dark mirror of the Material Plane, a realm of death and decay. In contrast, the Feywild is a realm of vibrant magic and natural beauty, where emotions and desires are amplified.

The Abyss and Other Planes:

The Abyss is an infinite plane of chaos and evil, home to demons and other malevolent entities. It is one of many outer planes, including the Nine Hells, Celestial Planes, and Elemental Planes, each with its unique characteristics and inhabitants.

Ancient Elven Mastery of Planar Travel:

Perhaps the most fascinating aspect of planar travel is the legendary mastery achieved by ancient elves. Through their profound understanding of magic and the fabric of reality, they developed the ability to manipulate planar boundaries at will. This mastery allowed them to open gates between planes and even restrict planar travel entirely, a feat that remains unmatched in modern times.

Artifacts of Planar Manipulation:

The ancient elves created various artifacts that facilitated their control over planar travel. These powerful items could create stable portals between planes or seal them off entirely. The exact nature and whereabouts of these artifacts remain a mystery, but their potential power continues to captivate scholars and adventurers alike.

Teleportation:

Teleportation is a form of magical transportation that allows instantaneous travel within a plane or between planes. It requires precise knowledge of destinations and carries risks if performed improperly. Advanced practitioners can transport groups or even entire structures across vast distances.

In conclusion, the study of planar travel and teleportation offers endless possibilities for exploration and discovery. As we continue to unravel the mysteries left behind by the ancient elves, we may one day reclaim the incredible mastery they once possessed over the boundaries between worlds.
