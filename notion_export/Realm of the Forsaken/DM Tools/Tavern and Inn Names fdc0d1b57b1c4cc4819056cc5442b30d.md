# Tavern and Inn Names

Tags: DM Tool

# 1. The Drunken Dragon

A lively tavern where the ale flows as freely as the tall tales. The establishment's sign features a tipsy dragon hiccuping small flames.

# 2. The Whispering Willow

A serene inn built around an ancient willow tree. Guests swear they can hear the tree's whispers in their dreams.

# 3. The Rusty Tankard

A no-frills pub known for its strong drinks and even stronger regulars. The bar top is adorned with countless dents from tankards slammed down in revelry.

# 4. The Gilded Goblin

An upscale tavern catering to wealthy merchants and nobles. Everything inside gleams with gold leaf, including the goblin-shaped door handles.

# 5. The Sleeping Giant

A cozy inn built into the side of a hill, rumored to be the resting place of a slumbering giant. The rooms occasionally rumble with its snores.

# 6. The Prancing Pegasus

A cheerful tavern popular with bards and storytellers. A magical illusion of a pegasus dances across the ceiling each night.

# 7. The Mystic Mermaid

A seaside inn decorated with salvaged ship parts and mermaid sculptures. The windows offer breathtaking views of the ocean.

# 8. The Wanderer's Rest

A welcoming roadside inn that caters to travelers and adventurers. The walls are covered in maps and mementos from far-off lands.

# 9. The Crimson Crow

A mysterious tavern known for its blood-red wine and secretive clientele. A large, intelligent crow serves as the establishment's mascot.

# 10. The Laughing Leprechaun

A boisterous Irish-themed pub where the beer is always green and the laughter never stops. Lucky patrons might spot the owner's "lucky" gold coin.

# 11. The Salty Siren

A rowdy dockside tavern frequented by sailors and pirates. The barmaid's enchanting voice can be heard over the din, calming even the roughest crowds.

# 12. The Moonlit Minotaur

A labyrinthine inn with corridors that seem to shift in the moonlight. Guests are given balls of string to find their way back to the main hall.

# 13. The Tipsy Troll

A cave-like tavern carved into the side of a cliff. The proprietor, a reformed troll, serves drinks in comically oversized mugs.

# 14. The Phoenix Feather

An elegant inn that burns down every full moon and rises from its ashes by morning. Guests are guaranteed a room that's always "like new."

# 15. The Enchanted Elixir

A whimsical tavern run by a retired alchemist. Every drink has a chance of producing a minor magical effect on the imbiber.

# 16. The Kraken's Cove

A dimly lit tavern decorated with salvaged ship parts and tentacle-shaped sconces. The specialty drink, "The Kraken's Kiss," is served smoking.

# 17. The Witching Hour

A tavern that only appears at midnight and vanishes at dawn. It's said that time passes differently inside, and a night of revelry might last mere minutes in the outside world.

# 18. The Gryphon's Nest

A cliffside inn offering stunning aerial views. Guests can rent trained gryphons for short flights around the area.

# 19. The Faerie's Fling

A whimsical tavern where the furniture is slightly too small and the drinks sparkle with fairy dust. Patrons often leave feeling unusually light and giggly.

# 20. The Wyrm's Whisker

A cozy tavern built inside the hollowed-out skeleton of an ancient dragon. The bar is crafted from a single, enormous dragon tooth.

# 21. The Alchemist's Abode

A peculiar inn where each room is themed after a different potion. The "Invisibility Suite" is notoriously hard to find.

# 22. The Basilisk's Gaze

A stone-walled tavern with an unusual gimmick: patrons must remain perfectly still when the barkeep shouts "Basilisk!" or face a silly penalty.

# 23. The Unicorn's Horn

A pristine, white-themed inn known for its healing hot springs. The spiral-shaped tower is visible for miles around.

# 24. The Sorcerer's Sanctuary

A magical pub where the glasses refill themselves and the fireplace changes color based on the overall mood of the patrons.

# 25. The Dragon's Hoard

A glittering tavern where everything is covered in (fake) gold and jewels. Patrons can pay extra for the "dragon's breath" spicy wing challenge.
