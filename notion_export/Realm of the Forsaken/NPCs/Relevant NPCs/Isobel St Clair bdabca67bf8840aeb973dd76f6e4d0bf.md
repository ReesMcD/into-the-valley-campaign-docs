# Isobel St. Clair

Species: Human
Status: Alive
Tags: NPC

- Plot Points
    - Rightful Heir to the throne in Ravell.
    - While a “princess,” she masqueraded as a vigilante knight and came to be known as “The Valkyrie” in her true Aasimir form.
    - Along with her mother, she opposed the NRTC, one of the few in the Nine Kingdoms to do so.
